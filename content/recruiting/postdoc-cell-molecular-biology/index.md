We are recruiting a **postdoctoral researcher in cell and molecular biology** — a biology-first
scientist who wants to pursue curiosity-driven research in an interdisciplinary team where
**AI and automation are everyday tools**, in the [AICell Lab](/about) at KTH and SciLifeLab.

You will design and run experiments and interpret results biologically, working shoulder to
shoulder with colleagues in computation and AI-assisted analysis. We shape the exact project
scope together at the start, so this description stays deliberately high-level. What matters most
is a strong foundation in cell, molecular or systems biology with hands-on experimental
experience; programming skills are a merit, not a requirement.

- **Reference:** PA-2026-2553
- **Application deadline:** 3 August 2026
- **Informal enquiries:** [wei.ouyang@scilifelab.se](mailto:wei.ouyang@scilifelab.se)
